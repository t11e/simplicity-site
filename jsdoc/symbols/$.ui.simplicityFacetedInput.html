Namespace $.ui.simplicityFacetedInput

Combines simplicityInputs and simplicityFacetCount.

Convenience wrapper around simplicityInputs and simplicityFacetCount.

The options for this widget are passed through to both of the contained widgets. Additionally, the option facetId is automatically created for the simplicityFacetCount widget from attributes on the wrapped input.
Defined in: simplicityFacetedInput.js.
